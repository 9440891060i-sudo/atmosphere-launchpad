Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Atmosphere ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Atmosphere mobile application (the "App"), a social platform that enables users to discover, share, and engage with content and communities based on their interests and geographic location. The App allows users to create personal profiles, post user-generated content, interact with other users, and explore location-based features designed to connect people with events, places, and experiences nearby.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information when you use the Atmosphere App. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices. By downloading, installing, or using the Atmosphere App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with applicable data protection laws and regulations, including the requirements set forth by the Apple App Store Review Guidelines (sections 1.2, 5.1.1, and 5.1.2), and reflects our commitment to responsible data stewardship.

2. Information We Collect

In order to provide you with a seamless and personalized experience, Atmosphere may collect the following categories of information:

  • Name: We collect your first and last name when you create an account so that other users can identify you on the platform and so that we can personalize your experience within the App.
  • Email Address: Your email address is collected during the registration process and is used for account verification, password recovery, important service-related communications, and responding to support inquiries you may submit.
  • Profile Data: Any information you choose to add to your user profile—including but not limited to a profile picture, biography, interests, and other optional details—is collected and displayed as part of your public or semi-public profile within the App.
  • Location Data: With your explicit permission, Atmosphere collects your device's geographic location data. This information is used to power location-based features such as discovering nearby content, events, and other users. You may revoke location access at any time through your device settings.
  • User-Generated Content: Any content you create, upload, or share through the App—including text posts, images, videos, comments, and messages—is collected and stored on our servers in order to deliver the core functionality of the platform.
  • Device Information: We may automatically collect certain technical information about your device, including the device model, operating system type and version, unique device identifiers, app version, language preferences, and crash or diagnostic logs. This information helps us maintain, optimize, and improve the App's performance and stability across a wide range of devices.

We only collect information that is necessary to provide, maintain, and improve the services offered through the Atmosphere App. We do not collect any data beyond what is described in this Privacy Policy without obtaining your prior consent.

3. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing App Features: Your information enables us to deliver the core features of the Atmosphere App, including account creation, profile management, content sharing, social interactions, and location-based discovery.
  • Maintaining User Accounts: We use your information to create, secure, and manage your user account, including verifying your identity, processing login requests, and facilitating password recovery.
  • Displaying Nearby Content: When you grant location permission, we use your geographic data to show you relevant content, events, places, and users in your vicinity, enhancing the location-based discovery features of the App.
  • Improving the Service: We analyze aggregated and anonymized usage data to understand how users interact with the App, identify areas for improvement, fix bugs, and develop new features that better serve our community.
  • Ensuring Safety and Compliance: We use information to detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent, abusive, or otherwise harmful activity on the platform, and to enforce our Terms of Use and community guidelines.
  • Communicating with You: We may use your email address to send you important service-related notifications, security alerts, and responses to your support requests. We do not send unsolicited marketing emails.

4. Data Storage

All personal information and user-generated content collected through the Atmosphere App is stored on secure servers operated by trusted third-party cloud infrastructure providers. We employ industry-standard security measures—including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and regular security audits—to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. When your data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it in accordance with our data retention policies.

We do not sell your personal data. Your information is never sold, rented, leased, or otherwise commercially transferred to third parties for their own marketing or advertising purposes.

5. Data Sharing

Atmosphere is committed to protecting your privacy and limiting the sharing of your personal information. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to any third party for any reason.

We may share limited information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App (such as cloud hosting and analytics), solely for the purpose of providing and improving our services. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only as directed by us.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.
  • Safety and Enforcement: We may share information as necessary to investigate or address violations of our Terms of Use, suspected fraud, or situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person.

In all cases, we share only the minimum amount of information necessary to fulfill the stated purpose.

6. Tracking

Atmosphere respects your privacy and is committed to a transparent, non-invasive approach to data collection.

We do not use advertising tracking. The Atmosphere App does not integrate with any third-party advertising networks, and we do not serve targeted advertisements based on your personal information or browsing behavior.

We do not track users across other apps or websites. We do not use cross-app or cross-site tracking technologies, nor do we participate in any advertising identifier programs designed to track user activity outside of the Atmosphere App.

Any analytics data we collect is used solely to improve the performance, stability, and user experience of the Atmosphere App, and is processed in an aggregated and anonymized manner wherever possible.

The Atmosphere App allows users to create, upload, and share content including text, images, videos, and other media ("User-Generated Content" or "UGC"). By posting content on the platform, you acknowledge that it may be visible to other users in accordance with your privacy settings and the nature of the content.

You are solely responsible for the content you post. You agree not to upload, share, or distribute any content that is illegal, abusive, harassing, defamatory, obscene, or otherwise in violation of our Terms of Use or community guidelines.

Atmosphere actively monitors the platform for abusive, harmful, or inappropriate content. We reserve the right to review, moderate, and remove any content that violates our policies, applicable laws, or the rights of other users. We also reserve the right to suspend or terminate the accounts of users who repeatedly or egregiously violate our content guidelines.

If you encounter content that you believe is inappropriate, harmful, or in violation of our policies, you may report it directly within the App using the built-in reporting feature. All reports are reviewed promptly by our moderation team.

8. Account Deletion

Atmosphere provides users with the ability to delete their accounts directly within the App at any time, without the need to contact support or submit a separate request. The account deletion feature is accessible through the App's settings menu.

When you choose to delete your account, the following actions are taken:

  • Your personal information—including your name, email address, profile data, and location data—is permanently removed from our active servers.
  • All user-generated content you have posted, including text, images, videos, and comments, is deleted from the platform.
  • Your account credentials are invalidated, and you will no longer be able to log in using the deleted account.

Please note that some residual data may remain in our backup systems for a limited period of time before being permanently purged, in accordance with our data retention and backup policies. Additionally, content that has been shared or reposted by other users may persist independently of your account deletion.

Account deletion is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered, and any data associated with it cannot be restored.
