Atmosphere Account Deletion Request

If you would like to delete your Atmosphere account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to: atmosphereplatform@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address or username associated with your Atmosphere account.

Our team will verify your request and permanently delete your account within 7 days.

Data that will be deleted:

  • User profile information
  • Messages and interactions
  • Account login credentials
  • Associated personal data

Data that may be retained temporarily:

  • Security logs for fraud prevention (retained for up to 30 days)

Once deletion is completed, your account cannot be recovered.
